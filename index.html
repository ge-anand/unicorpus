Unicorpus Health Foundation

Transforming Healthcare with Compassion and Innovation

What the Organization Is

Unicorpus is a not-for-profit healthcare organization that brings together diverse medical professionals and resources to serve those in need. Registered as a Section 25 company in India in 2015, it was structured to create a lasting impact beyond its founding team.

Core Mission and Vision

Their vision is to alleviate suffering by uniting diverse medical gifts into one body of care. Their mission includes:

  • Serving patients
  • Supporting employees
  • Contributing to society
  • Advancing technology, training, and research

Mottos: "Each one for the benefit of all" and "Know and own your health".

What They're Accomplishing

  • Clinical Impact: 28,000+ unique patients, 800+ inpatients, 360+ surgeries
  • Comprehensive Care: Multi-specialty services including brain surgery, rehab, ophthalmology, dental
  • Community Reach: 90+ health camps, telemedicine, COVID programs
  • Education: Newsletters, patient education, training programs
  • Facilities: Polyclinics, 30-bed hospital, emergency, pharmacy, specialty clinics
